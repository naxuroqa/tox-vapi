ErrSendFrame

Description:

public enum ErrSendFrame

Namespace: ToxAV
Package: toxcore

Content:

Enum values:

  • OK - The function returned successfully.
  • NULL - In case of video, one of Y, U, or V was NULL. In case of audio, the samples data pointer was NULL.
  • FRIEND_NOT_FOUND - The friend_number passed did not designate a valid friend.
  • FRIEND_NOT_IN_CALL - This client is currently not in a call with the friend.
  • SYNC - Synchronization error occurred.
  • INVALID - One of the frame parameters was invalid. E.g. the resolution may be too small or too large, or the audio sampling rate may be unsupported.
  • PAYLOAD_TYPE_DISABLED - Either friend turned off audio or video receiving or we turned off sending for the said payload.
  • RTP_FAILED - Failed to push frame through rtp interface.
